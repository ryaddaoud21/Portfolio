Project Details

Project information

  • Category: E-commerce WebSite
  • Client: Stella tech
  • Project date: 01 March, 2021
  • Technologies:
    • front-end front-end : HTML - CSS - JAVASCRIPT
    • back-end : DJANGO - MYSQL

Stella Tech store in its electronic form offers two main aspects with several related functionalities.
Client aspect : Like an electronic store for potential customers, a feature-rich, easy-to-use customer aspect. features :

  • - Authentication cart management (add product to cart, delete)
  • - Wish management profile management (change password, reset password)
  • - Product filtering (by name, category, brand, storage, etc.)

Administrator aspect:
An interactive administrator aspect is also required to facilitate the properties of the platform or the administrator and these sub-administrators, the management of the store. FEATURES :
  • - Product management (add -modify - delete - archive)
  • - Catalog management
  • - Brand management
  • - Order management
  • - Management of sub-admins (added by the super admin)
